Welcome to the Lawsss of deee Universeeee! It is important to understand these set of laws when cultivating a wild juicy life because it gives you the conscious power to choose how you want to live your life. We live in an energetic universe where everything is both glowing and attracting at all times. The laws of the universe are always working whether we are conscious of them or not. Here are a few that resonate the most with me.

Law of Vibration

"Everything in the universe has a frequency and a vibration". We have the ability to create the vibrations we want to be. This is the idea of low vibes vs high vibes. The law of vibration can be applied to everything from emotions to the food you are eating. What has a higher vibration Cheetos or an apple?

Law of Attraction

Law of attraction is about cultivating everything you want inside of you to attract it to you. We are constantly creating through our thoughts. Everything happens twice, first in your mind and second in your reality. To gain more abundance, you must be thankful for all the abundance currently in your life.

The Law of Relativity

This law is about seeing beyond good vs bad and understanding the neutrality of things. This law reminds you that there are multiple perspectives on what is happening around you.
