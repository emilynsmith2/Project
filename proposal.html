Stay Wild Stay Juicy

WHAT

My website is about how to stay true to your wild side and filled with so much juicy life. I will provide resources that I have used that will hopefully resonate with other people. I want to make it fun and relatable, while not being super spiritual and "woo woo"


OBJECTIVE

The objective of my website is to get people thinking in a higher state of mind, to get them out of their small minds and into the vortex! This will help inspire people to live their lives to the fullest


AUDIENCE

My target audience is the type of person who has opened their heart to new possibilities. This person can be of any age, it just depends how open the person is to learning and receiving this new way of thinking. This could be someone who is just starting their soul journey, or someone who needs a reminder. Overall the target audience is made up of people who do not take themselves too seriously but want to leave the Earth better than when they entered it!
